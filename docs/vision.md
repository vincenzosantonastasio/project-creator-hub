# Vision

## Long-Term Vision

Project Creator Hub aims to become the central layer of the creator economy.

A place where users can follow creators across all platforms without fragmentation.

---

## The Future

In the future, users will not think in terms of platforms.

They will think in terms of creators.

---

## Evolution

The platform will evolve to include:

- Personalized feeds based on followed creators  
- Smart content filtering  
- Advanced discovery of new creators  
- Deeper integration with platforms  

---

## Philosophy

The goal is not to replace platforms.

The goal is to organize them.

---

## Impact

- Simplify digital life for users  
- Increase visibility for creators  
- Bring structure to a fragmented ecosystem  

---

## Final Goal

Build the infrastructure layer of the creator economy.
