# Project Creator Hub

Project Creator Hub is an open-source initiative designed to organize the creator economy.

The internet has changed.

People no longer follow platforms.  
They follow creators.

Creators publish content across many platforms, which makes it difficult for fans to keep up with everything they share.

Project Creator Hub aims to solve this problem by organizing creator content across platforms in a single structured environment.

---

## Core Idea

> **People follow creators, not platforms.**

Creators publish across many platforms:

- YouTube  
- TikTok  
- Instagram  
- Podcasts  
- Blogs  
- Newsletters  
- LinkedIn  

Fans want to follow the creator — not search across many apps.

Project Creator Hub organizes all creator content into one structured space where fans can easily follow everything a creator publishes.

---

## What the Platform Does

The platform allows users to:

- follow creators  
- view content across multiple platforms  
- catch up on missed content  
- save valuable content  
- rediscover videos, posts and podcasts instantly  
- discover new creators  

The platform acts as an **organizational layer for the creator economy**.

---

## Supporting Creators

Project Creator Hub is designed to support creators, not replace platforms.

Users are encouraged to follow creators on their official platforms.

The platform communicates this clearly with the message:

**Follow this creator everywhere to see everything they publish.**

This ensures that:

- creators gain followers  
- platforms receive traffic  
- fans receive a better experience  

---

## Supporting Platforms

The platform does not host content.

Instead it organizes creator content and redirects users to the original platforms.

This means:

- platforms keep their traffic  
- creators keep their audience  
- fans gain organization and discovery  

---

## Open Source Project

Project Creator Hub is being built as an open-source project with the help of the developer community.

The goal is to build the simplest and most effective way for fans to follow creators across the internet.

Developers who believe in the idea are welcome to contribute to the project.

This is a community-driven initiative.

## Why This Project Exists

The idea for Project Creator Hub came from a simple observation.

Today creators publish content everywhere.

A single creator might publish on:

- YouTube  
- TikTok  
- Instagram  
- Podcasts  
- Blogs  
- Newsletters  

For fans this creates a problem.

Content becomes fragmented across many platforms and it becomes difficult to follow everything a creator publishes.

Many valuable pieces of content are lost simply because fans cannot remember where they were published.

Project Creator Hub was created to solve this problem by organizing the digital presence of creators into a single structured environment.
